Pet Skin Care

What is good for dogs skin and coat?

Flaxseed oil and fish oil are good sources of omega-3 fatty acids. To relieve itching or inflamed skin, owners should look for supplements that contain EPA (eicosapentaenoic acid) and DHA (docosahexaenoic acid).

How do I make my dog's coat shiny?

To make a dog's coat shine, feed it a healthy and nutritionally-rich diet. You can also add an omega-6 supplement to your dog's diet to help it develop plush, shiny fur. Groom your dog at least 1-2 times per week, or more often if it has long fur, using brushes and combs designed for dogs.

What is the best supplement for dogs with itchy skin?

Besides calming itchiness, EFAs correct dry skin, flakiness, and excessive shedding, to beautify and nourish the coat. Our favorite skin and coat supplements are Wisely Pollock Oil, Lifeline salmon oil, Nature's Logic sardine oil, Animal Essentials fish oil capsules and Ocean Omega.
